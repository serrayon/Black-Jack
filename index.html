Black Jack

Instructions

Its you versus the digital dealer! The goal is to get 21 or as close to without going over. You will be given begin with two cards, you must then decie if you want want to "Hit", if so the dealer will draw one more card from the deck. If you are happy with your number you may "Stand" and then the dealer will have his turn. All number cards carry a point value equal to the number they represent any suited card will count as ten.
