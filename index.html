Black Jack

Instructions

Its you versus the digital dealer! The goal is to get 21 or as close to without going over. You will be given two cards initially you must then decie if you want want to "Hit", if so the dealer will draw one more card from the deck. All number cards carry a point value equal to the number they represent any suited card will count as ten. The only exception is the Ace which can be worth either 1 or 11. First to ten points wins and in the case of draw both dealer and player will be awarded one point.
